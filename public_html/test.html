Tapenade (Olive Spread)

This is a really simple dish to preparate and it's always a big hit at parties. My father recommends:

"Make this the night before so that the flavors have time to blend. Just bring it up to room temperature before you serve it in winter, try serving it warm"

Ingredients

  • 1 8oz. jar sundried tomatoes
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 2/3 c. kalamata olives
  • 1 t. capers

Instructions

  1. Combine tomatoes and galic in a food procceros. Blend until as smooth possible.
  2. Add capers and olives. Pulse the motor a few times until they are incorporated, but still retain some texture.
  3. Serve on thin toast rounds with goat cheese and fresh basil garnish (optional).
